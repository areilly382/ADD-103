This summer I went on my first mission trip. We went to Ft. Lauderdale and built houses with Habitat for Humanity. It was so incredibly fun. We got to nail tin tags down as well as put up shingles during spring break. We went swimming every day and my hair quickly turned from orange to peach. We also went to our first Buckee's as a group which was so incredibly fun. I had an aboslute blast of a time and all though I don't consider myself religious, I am glad to went on the mission trip.
