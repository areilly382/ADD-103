About me Crochet Painting

It's everywhere!

I am constantly creating. Either drawing or writing or anything. I love to leave a bit of my creations with my friends. My biggest example is this little mushroom design seen in the image. About two or three years ago, I drew it on a friend using henna and he said that he would get it tattooed. Everyone thought it was a joke but here it is in fruition. We don’t talk anymore but the fact that he still did it meant a lot. It’s a crazy thought to have your art permanently on someone. Although I make visual art way less often than I used to, it’s still one of my favorite things. It’s my go to when I’m bored, overwhelmed, or just need an escape. I think a lot faster than I can produce though, so I constantly have unfinished work in projects. Right now, I have three canvasses of half-finished paintings. Not to even mention the amount of unfinish work in my sketchbooks. I also love to mess around with makeup and consider it painting cause that's how I view it. Makeup is just painting on my face.
